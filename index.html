Molly, my beemomma sona

cuddles every night, romantic and sweet like honey, produces 5 gal/day, bee sauce enjoyer, low maintenance but loves you

Body

She has a round belly and tail engorged with honey that she produces. Because of her weight she bumbles into walls when flying. Yes, she's human sized.

Her belly is fuzzy yet translucent; now how does that work?

Weaponry

Honey Handcannon Deals 5-7 damage and applies the effect Sticky for 1 turn.

Diet

Sometimes, she gets too hungry and dips her fingers in the honey — it usually ends up becoming whole handfuls instead of just finger samples though.

If she gets too big and can't move very well she can regurgitate the honey she produces. She doesn't like to waste though, so you might have to grab some containers.
