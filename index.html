Skinwalkers

Skinwalkers are a Class-A anomalous entity in the category Homoanalogae. They have the ability to — after a short period of observation — mimic the speech of humans it encounters. They resemble animals and in rare instances humans, albeit with notable deformities.

Skinwalkers have been shown to not pass the Boering-Rieler Test of consciousness.

Behaviour

Skinwalkers — like humans and their ancestors — are endurance hunters, pursuing their prey to the point of exhaustion where they are then killed and consumed.
