///Boering_Rieler_Test///
    <h1>Boering-Rieler Test</h1>
    
    <p>The <strong>Boering-Rieler Test</strong>, devised by <a>Charlie Boering</a> and <a>Jane Rieler</a>, attempts to test whether a living creature is aware of its existence/has conscious experience. A creature that fails the test is known as a <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philosophical_zombie">philosophical zombie</a> (or "p-zombie").</p>
    <p>Rieler used the Boering-Rieler Test as the basis for her <a href="Doll_machine">doll machine</a> thought experiment.</p>

///Andridae///
    <h1>Andridae</h1>
    
    <p><strong>Andrids</strong> (category <i>Andridae</i>) are a broad categorization of six-toed, arboreal, quadrupedal <a>anomalous entities</a>.</p>

    <h2>Appearance</h2>
    <p>Each foot has six digits: five slender toes and a pseudo-thumb evolved to better grasp tree limbs.</p>
    <p>All andrids are quadrupedal with the ability to rear up onto their hind legs to survey their environment or to appear larger.</p>
