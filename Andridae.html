Andridae

Andrids (category Andridae) are a broad categorization of six-toed, arboreal, quadrupedal anomalous entities.

Appearance

Each foot has six digits: five toes and a pseudo-thumb evolved to better grasp tree limbs.

All andrids are quadrupedal with the ability to rear up onto their hind legs to survey their environment or to appear larger.
