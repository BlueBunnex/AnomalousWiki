Conlang

Very simple language intended for habitual practice/use (by me). SOV word order.

Labial Alveolar Palatal Velar Glottal
Nasal m n ng
Plosive p t k
Fricative f v s h
Liquid w l j
Nouns
  • ya | food; wellness
  • ma | mouth
  • pi | eye
  • hasa | human
Verbs
  • ? | to be
  • yalang | to eat; to do well, to succeed
  • malang | to speak, to say
  • pilang | to see
Case Particles
  • i | Nominative (follows the subject of a sentence)
    ex. Hasa i malang. "A person speaks."
  • na | Accusative (follows the object of a sentence)
    ex. Hasa i ya na yalang. "A person eats food."
Derivational Affixes
  • -lang | noun to verb
